Manos and Maria

Who we are

With over 3 decades in business

Owning and operating a number of business across multiple industries Manos & Maria have a proven history in business. Together they serve as founders & directors of The Eview Group known for its unique real estate model. Due to their vast real estate knowledge Manos & Maria are regularly asked to participate in the real estate speaking circuit to share ideas on how they have created on of Australia's most exciting real estate groups. Thir life mission is 'To inspire and help others create a fun & profitable business so that they can live extraordinary lives.'
