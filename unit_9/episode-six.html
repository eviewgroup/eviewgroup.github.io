Episode 6 - Naomi Strong

Uploaded - April 16th 2017

On this weeks episode of 'On the couch with M&M' Manos & Maria interview Naomi Strong, Director & Founder of Strong Consulting Services, specialist real estate Trust Auditors & Accountants.


In an often neglected topic, Naomi shares her experience and gives advice on the correct operational procedures to effectively operate and meet statutory obligations of a real estate practice & Trust Account compliance.


Naomi's expertise extends to real estate business operations, Sales & Rental Trust Auditing, Rent Roll Acquisitions, Fraud investigations, Consumer Affairs & Business Licensing and internal compliance auditing.


Her passion for the industry and those running real estate businesses has set her on a path and desire to create best practice training programs and lift minimum qualifications for all participants in real estate administration.


We hope you enjoy this episode, (as much as Manos loves the topic of Trust Accounting!) and please share the post and leave comments and any further questions you may have!
