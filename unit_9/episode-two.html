Welcome to the latest episode of ‘On the Couch with M&M.’


In today’s interview, we find out why Amanda Newton, Director of the award-winning accountancy practice, Negotiis Accounting in Point Cook Melbourne, would venture into the world of real estate. This is a fascinating story of how after listening to their client’s challenges and disappointments with their external real estate transactions, inspired Negotiis to create a new generation of real estate service delivery.


Combining their award winning formula used in their accountancy firm and financial expertise, they hope to bring that same personal high touch experience through their soon to launch real estate division, Negotiis Property. This will certainly be a ‘watch this space’ journey.



Eview Group Real Estate are passionate about delivering outstanding sales and rental results. It would be our privilege to sell or lease your home and maximise your profit.


Our agents are Sales Professionals who have a vested interest in the success of the company. They take pride in the Eview brand and have made it their own.


We have strong affiliations with mortgage brokers, financial planners, utility connection providers and conveyancers. In simple terms, we make it easy for people to buy and sell. By providing a complete service and developing a long-term relationship, we like to earn the right to be referred to your family and friends.
