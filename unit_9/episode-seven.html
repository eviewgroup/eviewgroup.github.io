Welcome to another edition of ‘On the Couch with M&M.’


In this episode, we investigate a rather complex aspect of business ownership, that being ‘insurance.’ Our guest speaker and industry expert is long-time friend and service provider to the Eview Group, insurance broker Craig Wells. With 34 years of industry experience and knowledge, Craig navigates the many types of insurance products available to the business owner. How they apply to the business and the ramifications of being underinsured. This is an extensive look into the requirements of a real estate practices insurance needs, from a single operator agent to a full service real estate sales and property management business.
